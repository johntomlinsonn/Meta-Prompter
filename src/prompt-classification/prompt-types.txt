# ChatGPT Prompt Use-Case Taxonomy

## 1. Informational & Factual Retrieval
- **general_knowledge**: fact-based Q&A  
- **domain_specific_knowledge**: expert-level data or definitions  

## 2. Instructional & Tutorial
- **step_by_step_guidance**: multi-step instructions  
- **how_to_simplified**: "Explain like I'm 5" style instructions  

## 3. Analytical & Comparative
- **comparison_analysis**: pros/cons or side-by-side comparisons  
- **cause_effect_analysis**: reasoning on impact or consequences  

## 4. Text Generation & Writing
- **blog_article_writing**: structured long-form content  
- **social_media_copy**: short posts for platforms  
- **email_letter_drafting**: formal or informal messages  

## 5. Summarization & Synthesis
- **text_summarization**: bullet or paragraph summaries  
- **report_synthesis**: aggregate multi-source documents  

## 6. Classification & Extraction
- **text_classification**: label sentiment/topic  
- **entity_recognition**: extract named entities  
- **data_extraction**: parse structured information  

## 7. Coding & Technical
- **code_generation**: create code or scripts  
- **bug_fixing_debugging**: identify & correct errors  
- **technical_explanation**: clarify programming concepts  

## 8. Creative & Content Generation
- **storytelling_fiction**: original narrative writing  
- **poetry_lyrics**: poems or song lyrics  
- **ideation_brainstorming**: name or idea generation  

## 9. Multimodal & Media Support
- **image_description_analysis**: interpret visuals  
- **transcription_captioning**: convert audio/video to text  

## 10. Business & Productivity
- **customer_support_response**: draft replies  
- **market_research_summary**: analyze trends  
- **plan_strategy_development**: workflow or roadmap creation  

## 11. Personal Assistance
- **meal_planning**: recipes and grocery lists  
- **travel_itinerary_planning**: trip schedules & tips  
- **daily_schedule_management**: task planning & reminders  

## 12. Professional & Expert Roleplay
- **persona_based_advice**: expert guidance role-based  
- **simulation_roleplay**: interactive scenario execution  

## 13. Financial Advice & Planning
- **budgeting_finance_planning**: debt repayment or investing guidance  
- **side_hustle_planning**: business or additional income idea generation  

## 14. Emotional / Mental Support
- **therapy_roleplay**: empathetic conversational guidance  
- **life_coaching_advice**: supportive personal or relationship advice  

## 15. Educational Assistance
- **homework_help**: tutoring, problem walkthroughs  
- **lesson_plan_creation**: create educational materials  

## 16. Human Resources & Recruitment
- **job_description_creation**: write or refine postings  
- **interview_preparation**: generate Q&A and mock scenarios  

## 17. Translation & Language Services
- **text_translation**: convert text across languages  
- **language_learning_assistance**: practice and error correction  

## 18. Data Analysis & Reporting
- **data_summary_insights**: interpret spreadsheets or tables  
- **report_generation**: structured statistical or narrative summaries  

## 19. Legal & Compliance Support
- **contract_drafting**: generate template agreements  
- **policy_summarization**: digest legal or policy documents  

## 20. Healthcare & Medical Assistance
- **medical_info_summary**: non-professional medical guidance  
- **clinical_decision_support**: structured informational prompts for clinical scenarios
